Work

Domain Registration: One of Tech Solutions' primary services is domain registration. They offer domain registration for all major extensions, including .com, .net, .org, and many more. They also provide domain management tools that make it easy for businesses to manage their domains and make changes whenever necessary.

Web Space: Tech Solutions offers web space to businesses of all sizes. Their web space plans are designed to fit the needs of small start-ups as well as large corporations. They offer various web space plans that include unlimited bandwidth, email accounts, and other features that businesses need to run their websites.

SSL Certificate: Tech Solutions provides SSL certificates that ensure that websites are secure and safe to use. They offer different types of SSL certificates, including Domain Validated SSL, Organization Validated SSL, and Extended Validation SSL. This ensures that businesses have the right SSL certificate for their specific needs.

Email: Tech Solutions provides email services that businesses can use to communicate with their customers. Their email services include POP3 and IMAP support, spam filtering, and virus protection. This ensures that businesses can communicate with their customers effectively and securely.

Website Designing: Tech Solutions offers website designing services to businesses that need to create a new website or redesign an existing one. They have a team of professional website designers who can create stunning websites that are responsive and user-friendly. Their website designing services also include SEO optimization to ensure that websites rank high in search engine results.

Web Content: Tech Solutions provides web content services that businesses can use to create engaging and informative content for their websites. Their web content services include content writing, copywriting, and editing. This ensures that businesses have high-quality content that is optimized for search engines and engages their audience.

VOIP Solution: Tech Solutions provides VOIP solutions that businesses can use to communicate with their customers. Their VOIP solutions include features like call recording, conference calling, and auto-attendant. This ensures that businesses can communicate with their customers effectively and efficiently.

Custom Web and Mobile App Development: Tech Solutions offers custom web and mobile app development services to businesses that need to create custom applications that meet their specific needs. Their team of developers can create applications that are responsive, user-friendly, and designed to fit the specific needs of businesses. They also provide ongoing support to ensure that applications are always up-to-date and running smoothly.
